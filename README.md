# Altitude

**Transforming travel booking into a seamless social experience.**

## About

Altitude is an AI-powered travel planning and booking platform built by Altitude
Travel Ltd. Our mission is to make every journey effortless, trustworthy, and
socially connected. We envision a world where travel planning is as enjoyable as
the trip itself.

## What we build

We are building a cross-device platform — available on the web and iOS — that
brings together:

- **Natural-language trip planning** — Describe your ideal trip in your own
  words and let our Peregrine engine handle the rest.
- **Integrated booking** — Plan and book flights, accommodation, and activities
  in one place with transparent pricing and a simple 5% markup.
- **Social travel** — Share itineraries, collaborate on group trips, and
  discover destinations through your network.

Whether you are a solo traveller, a family, or a business, Altitude is designed
to fit the way you travel.

## Our approach

We build on open standards, including the Model Context Protocol (MCP), to
create a platform that is interoperable, privacy-first, and built to last. We
believe great travel technology should work for you — not the other way around.

## Community

We host meetups at the intersection of travel, technology, and community
building. If that sounds like your kind of thing, we would love to see you
there.
